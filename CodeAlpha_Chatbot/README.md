# CodeAlpha_Task_2_ChatBot-for-FAQs-

Introducing ShopBot AI — an AI-driven chatbot built to transform how your e-commerce customers find answers. No more long wait times, no more unanswered queries, no more frustrated shoppers leaving your store empty-handed.

At its core, ShopBot AI is powered by a Natural Language Processing engine built with cosine similarity matching — a proven mathematical technique that understands what your customers are truly asking, not just the exact words they typed. Whether a customer asks "where is my package" or "track my order please," ShopBot knows exactly what they need and delivers the right answer instantly.

What makes ShopBot AI stand out?

It speaks your customers' language — literally. ShopBot is fully bilingual, supporting both English and Bangla. It auto-detects the language with zero configuration and responds accordingly.

It runs around the clock, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week — so your business never misses a customer question, even at 3 in the morning.

It covers the full customer journey — from placing an order to tracking deliveries, payment methods, return policies, promo codes, and account management.

It learns and grows. The built-in Admin Panel lets you add, edit, and bulk-upload new FAQ entries in real time — no developer needed.

It is beautiful and modern. Built with a dark AI-inspired interface, smooth animations, and a responsive design that works flawlessly on any device.

ShopBot AI is built on Python and Flask, using Tailwind CSS for the frontend — making it lightweight, fast, and easy to deploy on any server. It requires no paid AI subscription, no monthly API costs, and no external dependency. Everything runs locally, keeping your customer data private and your costs at zero.

In a world where customers expect instant answers, ShopBot AI gives your business a competitive edge — delivering the experience of a 24/7 support team, without the overhead of one.

This is not just a chatbot. This is your always-on, always-smart, always-helpful shopping companion. This is ShopBot AI.
